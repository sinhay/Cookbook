Breakfast

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 cups of flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of oil

Directions

  • Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
  • Add all wet ingredients to the bowl and mix thoroughly
  • Scoop some batter into a pan or griddle and flip once golden brown
  • Cook the otherside until also golden brown
  • Enjoy with desired toppings

Fried Egg

Ingredients

  • Eggs, as many as desired
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hot Sauce
  • Small glass of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil per 3 eggs

Directions

  • Place frying pan on stove-top above a medium flame
  • Wait for frying pan to reach temperature
  • While waiting for pan to reach temperature, in a separate bowl crack in eggs, being careful not to break yolk
  • Remove any shells from bowl
  • Once at temperature put in appropriate amount of vegetable oil
  • Transfer eggs from bowl into pan
  • After two minutes or until egg whites begin to fry, pour half a small glass of water into pan then cover pan with lid
  • Let eggs steam in pan anywhere from 10-30 seconds
  • Uncover pan, and remove pan from heat
  • Turn off stove and let eggs rest in pan for 30 seconds
  • Plate eggs and season with salt, pepper, and hot-sauce

Vitamin C Water

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dissolvable vitamin c tablets

Directions

  • Get a water bottle or glass
  • Pour in desired amount of water
  • Get 1 vitamin c tablet and drop it into water
  • Wait for it to dissolve then drink and enjoy your immune system not getting weakened

Buttered Toast

Ingredients

  • Slice of bread
  • 1/4 tablespoon of salted butter

Directions

  • Place your slice of bread in the toaster until it reaches your desired doneness
  • Remove toast from the toaster and spread the butter on the toast with a knife
  • Enjoy!
