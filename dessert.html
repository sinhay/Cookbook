Dessert

Sugar Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/2 a stick of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Sliced bread

Directions

  • In a small bowl, mix together the butter and sugar until you get a spreadable consistency
  • Spread on bread slices and toast until golden brown

Ice Cream Sundae

Ingredients

  • Ice cream flavor of your choice
  • Sprinkles
  • Fudge
  • Cherry

Directions

  • Using the ice cream scoop place your desired amount of ice cream into a bowl
  • Start by drizzling fudge on top of the ice cream
  • Decorate with sprinkles
  • Top with a cherry
